The first thing you see when you awaken is a crow picking the eyeball out of dead, half eaten man. A foul smell permeates the air, after a moment or two you discover that its coming from you.
Sitting up very slowly you take a brief glance around. Your in a forest no doubt. Straight in front of you is a deep heavyset tree. 
Its countless branches span outward to form a blanket of leaves overhead through which a faint trickle of moon light flows through barley illuminating your surrondings.
Reaching down to grab your sword you feel the pommel comfortingly rasp against your knuckles. At least that's there you think.
Fear starts to course through your veins, turning your heart cold when you realize the full gravity of the situation.
All around you are your slain comrades, mercilessly slashed open and torn apart. There are also many others such as the man with no eyeball, rotted and missing limbs and internals.
Unable to stop yourself you double over and vomit all over the ground. That's when you hear a twig snap and an ominous growling start behind you.
With extreme caution you turn around to stare directly into a huge werewolf's eyes. Your bowels loosen and you nearly release your inards.

The werewolf is about seven and a half feet tall with a huge barreled chest. His arms and legs bulge with muscle and sinew, and from his mouth a layer of drool slowly trickles out off his razor sharp teeth.
Each tooth is about four inches long with two larger canines protruding over its lips.
His entire coat is as black as midnight and his eyes glow a vicious yellow.

What do you do?
