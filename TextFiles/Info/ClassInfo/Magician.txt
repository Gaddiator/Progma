The Magician focuses on all things arcane, using powerful offensive and defensive spells to wreak havoc.
They have a very high intelligence which comes in handy in many crafty areas and situations.
While rather weak in close quarter combat, they make up for it with a vast inventory of spells and devastating offensive magic.
Overall they are a extremely powerful class, and if used correctly very rewarding to play.
