The Rouge focuses on a vast array of specialist skills such as climbing, lock picking, long weapons, speech and much more.
They have a high intelligence and dexterity making them quick and craftily dangerous.
While a formidable opponent at long, medium, and close range combat there are other who are better.
This makes them need to utilise their speed, intelligence and their vast array of skills to give them the advantage.
Overall they are a very versatile balanced class and extremely rewarding to play if at times challenging.
