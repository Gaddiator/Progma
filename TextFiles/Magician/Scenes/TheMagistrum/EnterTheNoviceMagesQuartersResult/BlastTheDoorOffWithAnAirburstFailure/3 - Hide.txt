You quickly slide over to the corner of the room which you saw the barrels at earlier and duck behind them, leaving just enough room to spy over their edges and look toward the door.
Without warning it comes flying off the hinges with explosive force, accelerating into the room with such speed and power that it travels almost twenty feet from where it had originally been before stopping, landing with a thud by the smashed windows.

Tearing your eyes from the door your gaze returns to gaping hole in the wall where it was but a few seconds before.
