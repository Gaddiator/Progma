The first thing you see when you wake up is a dead novice mage, his limbs broken and hanging limp, sticking out at all angles. The result of a fierce magical attack no doubt.
Looking around you see similar atrocities. Bodies lay scattered and torn apart, you can even see some young magicians who are frozen solid with ice magic just as some others are burnt to the same texture as a well roasted steak.
The bookshelves in the library are mostly destroyed, charred tome's littered everywhere and all of the smashed apothecary tools and glass jars are letting of an acrid  yellow and brown coloured smoke.
Windows mostly smashed and doors lay blown off their hinges the snow slowly creeping through the gap along with the faintest glimmer of moonlight. The only source of light.

Fallen masonry covers most of your body obscuring you from sight, and you struggle to work your way free.
After a couple of tense seconds wiggling to and fro you manage to pop a shoulder out, followed by your arm.
After that its only a matter of time before you free your other arm and then pull your legs out from under the largest block.

Carefully you roll over into a crouch alert for danger, every one of your senses tingling and you feel ready to pounce.
After a handful of tense seconds you realise that you are in no imminent danger and you you start slowly scanning your surroundings.
It seems that no one else is alive, the wights and banes dealt with that you think grimly. Considering your options you begin to formulate a plan.
You can see the main door that leads outside and one more to your rear which leads out of the library, your current location and into the magistrum.
